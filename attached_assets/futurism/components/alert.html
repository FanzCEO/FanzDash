Alerts

Provide contextual feedback messages for typical user actions, offering a simple way to display important information and warnings.

Alerts

Primary: Here is additional information that may be useful.
Info: Here is additional information that may be useful.
Success! Operation completed successfully.
Danger! An error occurred, please review the entered data.
Warning! Please double-check the entered information.
Close! Please close this alert with click x icon. 
                  
<!-- Alert -->
<div class="py-2.5 px-4 rounded-sm text-sm flex gap-2 items-center justify-between border border-primary-600 bg-primary-950 text-primary-300">
  <div class="flex items-center gap-1">
    <strong class="text-primary-300">Primary:</strong> Here is additional information that may be useful.
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Info Alert -->
<div class="py-2.5 px-4 rounded-sm text-sm flex gap-2 items-center justify-between border border-sky-600 bg-sky-950 text-sky-300">

    <strong class="text-sky-300">Info:</strong> Here is additional information that may be useful.
  </div>
</div>

<div class="py-2.5 px-4 rounded-sm text-sm flex gap-2 items-center justify-between border border-green-600 bg-green-950 text-green-300">

    <strong class="text-green-300">Success!</strong> Operation completed successfully.
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Danger Alert -->

  </div>
</div>

<div class="py-2.5 px-4 rounded-sm text-sm flex gap-2 items-center justify-between border border-yellow-600 bg-yellow-950 text-yellow-300">

    <i class="bi bi-exclamation-circle me-2 text-yellow-300"></i>
    <strong class="text-yellow-300">Warning!</strong> Please double-check the entered information.
  </div>
</div>

<!-- Closeable Alert -->
<div x-data="{ open: true }" x-show="open" class="py-2.5 px-4 rounded-sm text-sm flex gap-2 items-center justify-between border border-primary-600 bg-primary-950 text-primary-300">
  <div class="flex items-center gap-1">
    <i class="bi bi-exclamation-circle me-2 text-primary-300"></i>
    <strong class="text-primary-300">Close!</strong> Please close this alert with click x icon.
  </div>
  <button @click="open = false"><i class="bi bi-x-lg text-primary-300"></i></button>
</div>
